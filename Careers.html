Be Part of Our Mission

At Safe101, we are dedicated to making the world a safer place for women. Our team is passionate, innovative, and driven by a common purpose: to empower women with the tools they need to feel secure and confident. If you're ready to make a difference, explore the opportunities below and join us in our mission.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in empowering women and providing them with the resources to stay safe.
  • Innovation: We are constantly innovating to stay ahead and offer the best safety solutions.
  • Community: We are a close-knit community, supporting each other and our users.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of integrity in everything we do.
  • Diversity: We value diversity and inclusivity, welcoming people from all backgrounds.
