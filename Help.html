Frequently Asked Questions

1. What should I do in case of an emergency?

Immediately use the Safe101 app to alert your emergency contacts and local authorities.

2. How can I contact Safe101 support?

You can reach out to our support team via email at support@safe101.com or call +91 1234567890.

3. Can I customize the safety features of the app?

Yes, Safe101 allows you to customize safety alerts and notifications according to your preferences.

4. How do I update my profile information?

Go to the 'Edit Profile' section in the app settings to update your personal information.

5. Is my data secure with Safe101?

Absolutely. We use advanced encryption methods to ensure the safety and privacy of your data.

6. How can I add or remove emergency contacts?

You can manage your emergency contacts through the 'Contacts' section in the app settings.

7. What is the 'Geolocation Tracker' feature?

The 'Geolocation Tracker' allows your trusted contacts to track your location in real-time during an emergency.

8. How do I report a problem with the app?

Please contact our support team via email or call us directly to report any issues you encounter.

9. Can I use the app offline?

Some features require an internet connection, but critical safety alerts can be activated offline.

10. How often is the app updated?

We regularly update the app to improve functionality and security. Ensure you have the latest version for optimal performance.
